Are you tired of being treated like you're a scammer or a thief? You're a savvy craigslist buyer/seller. You've conducted tons of transactions and you've never screwed anyone over. As a buyer you're prompt, you communicate clearly with the other party, and always have cash ready - with exact change! As a seller, you're there when & where you say you'll be, with the product exactly as described.

...but with each new transaction, you're reduced to a total stranger who just as easily *could* be a scammer or a thief.

With clrep, each time you conduct a transaction you can have the other party vouch for you and up your reputation. Think of it like your Ebay Feedback Score, but for craigslist. And once you've built up your reputation, you can even use your clrep badge in your craigslist ad to give comfort to the viewers that you're a worthy person to do business with.
